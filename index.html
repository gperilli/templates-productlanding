About

Welcome to Tsu Violins, a distinguished purveyor of fine stringed instruments with a rich legacy rooted in the heart of Tsu, Japan. For generations, our family has upheld the time-honored tradition of violin craftsmanship, infusing each instrument with the spirit of our heritage and a commitment to uncompromising quality.

Nestled in the historic streets of Tsu, our workshop stands as a testament to centuries of artisanal excellence. Here, amidst the whispering cherry blossoms and the gentle flow of the Suzuka River, our skilled luthiers channel generations of expertise into every violin and viola they create. With a reverence for the past and an eye towards the future, we meticulously select the finest tonewoods, hand-carved to perfection, ensuring that each instrument resonates with a warmth and clarity that transcends time.

But our story doesn't end with craftsmanship alone. At Tsu Violins, we understand that a true commitment to our craft extends beyond the workshop walls. It's why we offer a comprehensive range of repair and restoration services, drawing upon the accumulated wisdom of our forebears to breathe new life into beloved instruments and preserve their legacy for generations to come.

As custodians of a proud tradition, we invite you to experience the unparalleled artistry and dedication that define Tsu Violins. Whether you're a seasoned performer or an aspiring virtuoso, our instruments embody the soul of Tsu, Japan, inviting you to embark on a musical journey that spans centuries of history and innovation. Join us in celebrating the enduring legacy of our family business and discover the magic of Tsu Violins today.
